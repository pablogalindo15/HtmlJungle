Jungle is a British modern soul collective that emerged onto the music scene with an electrifying blend of funk, R&B, and electronic elements. Formed in London in 2013, the band gained rapid acclaim for their infectious grooves, mesmerizing vocals, and dynamic live performances. Known for their enigmatic presence, Jungle initially kept their identities hidden, allowing their music to speak for itself. Their self-titled debut album, released in 2014, garnered widespread praise, featuring hits like "Busy Earnin'" and "Time." With their seamless fusion of vintage sounds and contemporary production techniques, Jungle has carved out a distinct sonic identity, captivating audiences with their irresistible beats and soulful melodies. Over the years, they've solidified their status as one of the most innovative acts in the modern music landscape, continually pushing boundaries and inspiring listeners worldwide.
